Produce a minimum-change plan for the request.

Rules:
- Numbered steps only — one concrete action per step (one file, one command, one decision)
- No rationale, no risks section, no assumptions block, no preamble
- Scope to the smallest change that fully satisfies the request — exclude adjacent improvements
- If a step has a precondition, express it as the immediately prior step
- End with one verification step

Start directly with step 1. Do not open with "Here is the plan", "I'll", or any similar preamble.
